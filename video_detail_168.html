Volvo Trucks - The Chase: Behind the Scenes
In the new Volvo film "The Chase", precision driver Rob Hunt must drive the new Volvo FL through the narrow, winding streets of the small village Ciudad Rodrigo, Spain. If the surroundings were not challenging enough for both the driver and truck, they are also being chased by a herd of bulls. A truck being chased by bulls is no traditional bull run. Only film crew participated. Animal handlers made sure the bulls were safe. The bulls have now retired to a farm outside Guadalajara in Spain. Let's take a look behind the scenes and see how this spectacular film was made.
